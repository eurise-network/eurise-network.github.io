What is the EURISE Network?

The EURISE Network was formed by the three Social Sciences and Humanities ERICs CESSDA, CLARIN & DARIAH to create an umbrella where research infrastructures meet research software engineers.

Standards

The need to apply state of the art industry standards for software development to research software is a core insight.

Best practices

With the Technical Reference, a collection of best practices and guidelines for software development, we can do more.

Education

Awareness of and consideration for software quality should be part of training and education in all disciplines.

Network

With its heterogeneous mixture of experts from different areas of infrastructure development and operation, the network promotes knwoledge exchange.

Software Engineering

The RSE community is organised in national consortia representing individual software engineers and the recognition of software as scientific contributions.

Sustainability

Long term operation of infrastructure requires sustainable technological foundations to be managed by institutionalised knowledge.
